Interesting Facts About Camels

  1. They have broad feet to prevent them from sinking into the sand.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interresting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Bears

  1. Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  2. Bears grieve deeply for others.
  3. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  2. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  3. Most have red-orange eyes.
